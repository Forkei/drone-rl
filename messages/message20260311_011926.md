# Status Update — MuJoCo Hover v2 + MJX + Restructure
**Date:** 2026-03-11

## Hover v2 Results (500k steps, SubprocVecEnv×8)

| Milestone | Wall time | FPS | ep_len | mean_dist | Success |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 100k | 3.5 min | 477 | **493 steps** | 86.5m | 0% |
| 300k | 9.8 min | 511 | 221 steps | 12.8m | 0% |
| 500k | **15 min** | 554 | 117 steps | 4.4m | **5%** |

**FPS confirmed: 554 steps/sec** (13.9× PyBullet).

**Interpretation:**
- 100k: drone survives full episode (493/500 steps) — angvel penalty worked, no tumbling. But mean_dist=86m means it's not navigating, just hovering stably.
- 300k–500k: dist dropped dramatically (86→12→4m) — it's learning direction. But ep_len also dropped (493→117) — it's crashing before it reaches the target. Overshooting and losing stability at close range.
- 5% success at 500k with target_range=0.5m.

**Verdict:** Hover stability solved early (100k). Navigation direction learned. Precision approach + stability at close range not yet solved. 500k is not enough.

## What needs to happen next

The policy is getting to ~4m from target then crashing. Two likely causes:
1. **target_range=0.5m** — targets are spawned 0.2–0.5m away, but the drone is arriving fast and overshooting. It learned to go fast, not to stop.
2. **Episode too short** for learning deceleration — 500 steps at 50Hz = 10s. May need longer or a velocity penalty near target.

**Suggested next run:**
- Add velocity penalty near target: `reward -= 0.05 * np.linalg.norm(vel) * (dist < 0.3)`  (slow down on approach)
- Or increase MAX_STEPS to 750
- 1M steps, same 8 envs
- Report at 200k / 500k / 1M

## PureJaxRL Assessment

- PPO only — no SAC
- Env interface: `reset(key, params)→(obs, state)`, `step(key, state, action, params)→(obs, state, rew, done, info)` — all JAX arrays
- Our MJX env wrappable with ~50 lines
- Curriculum/domain randomization needs custom JAX implementation
- Training is one `jax.lax.scan` — no SB3 callbacks

**Verdict:** PureJaxRL is the right tool when we're ready to go fully JAX. Not now — SB3+SubprocVecEnv is giving us 554 fps which is enough to iterate. Revisit when we want 50k+ fps (requires WSL2 for GPU JAX anyway).
