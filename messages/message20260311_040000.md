### Key finding: HIGH_TARGET IS SOLVED
PyBullet's persistent 8/9 failure is gone in MuJoCo best_model. Direct motor control
handles pure vertical climb in 55 steps. nav_final regresses (0.15m, timeout) —
training instability at the end of the run. best_model is the keeper.

### One remaining gap: far_1.8m in best_model
best_model goes to 5.45m — badly overshoots. This is a curriculum artifact: best_model
was saved at 1.86M steps when curriculum just advanced to 2m range. Policy hadn't yet
stabilized at long range. nav_final solves it (83 steps). Could run a short fine-tune
from nav_final to fix high_target, but that risks forgetting far_1.8m again.
