## Episode structure answer
- **Control frequency**: 48 Hz (each `env.step()` = 1 control step)
- **Episode length**: 240 steps = 5 seconds at 48 Hz
- **Termination (terminated)**: dist < 0.0001 m to target — basically never fires, just a success flag
- **Truncation (truncated)**:
  - |x| > 1.5 or |y| > 1.5 or z > 2.0 m — flew out of bounds
  - |roll| > 0.4 or |pitch| > 0.4 rad — tipped over
  - step_counter/PYB_FREQ (240 Hz physics) > 5s — timeout
- At convergence with RPM: ep_len_mean = 240 (drone surviving full episodes without crashing)

The 5s / 240-step limit is fine for hover. For navigation with a 0.5m radius we may want to extend to 10s (480 steps) — flying to a random point and stabilizing needs more time. Easy to set via `EPISODE_LEN_SEC` kwarg on the env.
