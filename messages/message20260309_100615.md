# Update — Nav training running (fixed)

## Bugs found and fixed during nav bring-up

**Bug 1: Drone spawning at ground level**
HoverAviary spawns at z≈0.1m. Nav targets are around z=1.0m.
With random actions the drone couldn't bootstrap learning (needed takeoff + nav).
Fix: `initial_xyzs = home_pos` so drone starts at [0,0,1] already in the target zone.

**Bug 2: PID actions command underground flight**
ActionType.PID = 3D target in [-1,1]^3. Random z∈[-1,0] → drone flies underground → crash.
Fix: `step()` override clamps z to [0.15, 2.0] before passing to parent.

**Bug 3: -dist reward has no signal on short episodes**
With 20-step episodes and -0.1*dist reward, gradients are tiny and inconsistent.
Fix: switched to potential-based reward: `(prev_dist - curr_dist) * 2.0 - 0.01`
This gives positive signal for ANY progress toward goal, even on short episodes.
