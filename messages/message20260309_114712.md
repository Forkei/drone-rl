## Key learning: curriculum trigger
Original: training success_rate ≥ 0.60 → **never advances** (stochastic policy is 3× noisier than deterministic eval)
Fixed: EITHER training success_rate ≥ threshold OR eval_reward ≥ threshold (whichever first)
The `EvalCurriculumCallback` subclass feeds eval results back to `NavCallback`.
This design pattern is worth keeping for all future curriculum work.
